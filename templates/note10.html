Whenever life feels overwhelming, I want you to remember that I am always by your side, no matter what happens, no matter how lost you may feel. No matter how stressed, tired, or defeated you are, you are never truly alone. I’m here—not just to listen, but to hold you, to remind you that you are so strong, capable, and endlessly loved beyond what words could ever explain. No matter what anyone says, no matter what obstacles life throws your way, I will never be disappointed in you, no matter what happens in the future. You don’t have to prove anything to me—I’m already proud of you, always, in every single way, through every high and low, through every little thing. So when the weight of the world feels too much, come to me and lean on me, cry if you need to, talk to me, let me remind you of how strong you are. Let me remind you how far you’ve come, how much brighter the world feels because you're in it, how much I love you, and how I’ll always be here. Yours truly, always.
